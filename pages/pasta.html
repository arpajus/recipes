Pasta

Description

Experience the essence of Italian cuisine with our delightful pasta, a dish that embodies the traditions of Italy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of high-quality pasta
  • 1 pound of fresh, ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cup of finely grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  • Boil the pasta in a pot of water with half a tablespoon of salt for 12 â€“ 14 minutes until al dente.
  • Meanwhile, in a frying pan, heat olive oil and add fresh tomatoes.
  • Season the tomatoes with salt, garlic, and fine herbs, allowing them to cook for a delightful 10 minutes.
  • Drain the pasta, and delicately combine it with the flavorful tomato sauce.
  • Enhance the dish by garnishing with your favorite type of cheese, preferably Parmesan, for an irresistible flavor.
