Brownie

Description

Indulge in the decadent world of chocolate with our delightful brownie, a rich and satisfying dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of high-quality chocolate

Steps

  • In a saucepan, combine butter or margarine with chocolate chips over low heat. Stir often until melted; avoid overheating. Remove from heat and set aside on a hot pad.
  • In a mixing bowl, beat eggs until frothy. Add sugar and vanilla, beating to mix. Incorporate the melted chocolate mixture into this mixture. Stir until well combined.
  • Add flour and walnuts, stirring just until moistened.
  • Scrape the batter into a baking pan, spreading it evenly.
  • Bake until a toothpick inserted comes out with moist crumbs, not wet batter.
