Pizza

Description

Indulge in the art of simplicity with our classic pizza, a dish known for its ease of preparation and widespread popularity in Italy.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  • Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness.
  • Spread a generous layer of fresh tomatoes over the dough.
  • Arrange flavorful pepperoni slices and sprinkle with a generous amount of grated cheese.
  • Bake the pizza in the oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is perfectly melted.
